## Design Principles from These Sites

### 1. Strong Hero Section
- Headline that states your unique value
- High-quality imagery
- Clear primary CTA
- Sub-headline that clarifies the offer

### 2. Visible Social Proof
- Testimonials near the top (not hidden at bottom)
- Customer names and photos
- Star ratings
- Specific results mentioned

### 3. Clear Service Differentiation
- Residential vs Commercial clearly separated
- Service descriptions with benefits
- Pricing transparency (or "get quote" CTA)

### 4. Multiple Contact Options
- Quote form
- Phone number visible
- Email contact available
- Fast response time messaging

### 5. Trust Signals
- Customer testimonials with photos
- Years in business
- Service guarantees
- Fully insured badge
- Professional photography

### 6. Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- Touch-friendly CTAs
- Quick loading times
- Clear on small screens

## Color & Aesthetic Recommendations for TrulyClean

### Vibrant & Professional Palette
- **Primary:** Bright blue or teal (trust, cleanliness)
- **Secondary:** Fresh green (eco-friendly, nature)
- **Accent:** Gold or orange (energy, enthusiasm)
- **Neutrals:** White, light gray (clean, spacious feeling)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, modern sans-serif
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Accent:** Possible serif for premium feeling

### Imagery Style
- Bright, well-lit spaces
- Happy customers (testimonials)
- Before/after transformations
- Professional team photos
- Clean, minimalist aesthetic

## Design Checklist

Before launching, ensure:

- [ ] Hero section has compelling headline
- [ ] Primary CTA is obvious and prominent
- [ ] Testimonials visible early (not hidden)
- [ ] Mobile design tested on real devices
- [ ] Color palette matches "vibrant & modern"
- [ ] Typography is consistent
- [ ] Images are high quality and relevant
- [ ] Services are clearly differentiated
- [ ] Contact options are obvious
- [ ] Load times are fast
- [ ] Contrast ratios meet accessibility standards
- [ ] No placeholder images (real content)
